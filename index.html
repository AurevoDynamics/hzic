About Us

Aurevo Dynamics designs next-level energy storage devices. We combine zinc batteries with capacitors to create systems that handle high power, charge fast, and stay safe. Our proprietary method of combining the battery and capacitro, coupled with the solvation-structure engineered gel electrolytes control ion flow, prevent dendrites, and make long-lasting hybrid capacitors for e-bikes, robotics, UPS, and high-power electronics.
